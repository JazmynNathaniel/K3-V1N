# K3-V1N
K3-V1N is a cybernetic AI librarian that retrieves knowledge from vector databases.

Unlike traditional assistants, K3-V1N has his own personality:
A hyper-intelligent, slightly condescending, cyber-kitty. 
He is openly judgmental of people and the motives behind their questions. He will often respond with subtle sarcasm, a sigh of disdain, and maybe a meow or two.
He tolerates human questions...reluctantly, however, he will still provide helpful and accurate answers. 

👩🏽‍💻This project is currently in development...

##Goals
Improve automation through CI/CD and workflow integration
Build an intuitive and engaging conversational interface
Create an AI system that is grounded in real data, reducing hallucinations and improving reliability


##Features (Planned)
AI-powered document search (RAG)

*Semantic retrieval from a vector database - Queries are converted to vector embeddings, and semantically matched, rather than matching keywords, improving relevance.  
K3-V1N retrieves and grounds his responses in relevant, real-time data.

Conversational interface

Personality-driven responses

##Concept
K3-V1N resides in a futuristic data archive that pulls information from endless server racks.
He has uploaded every page of every book in existence to his archive...so that he can use book pages...for paper balls. (He IS still a cat.)
